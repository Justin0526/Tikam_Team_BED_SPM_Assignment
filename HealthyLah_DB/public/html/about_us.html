About Us

HealthyLah is a community-driven platform designed to empower seniors in Singapore to live healthier, happier lives. Our goal is to make everyday tasks like checking the weather, finding transport, managing health appointments, and enjoying hobbies easier and more enjoyable.

This website was created as part of a school project in collaboration with Lions Befrienders to support the needs of our senior community. By combining simple interfaces with useful tools, we help older adults stay informed, connected, and independent.

Whether you’re looking for nearby clinics, managing your medications, logging your meals, or just having fun with mini-games or journaling, HealthyLah has something to offer.

Developed by:
A passionate team of Year 2 InfoComm students at Ngee Ann Polytechnic.
Backend powered by Node.js & Express, integrated with SQL and RESTful APIs.
