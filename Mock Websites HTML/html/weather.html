ℹ️ What Do These Numbers Mean?

What is the UV Index?

0-2: Low – Minimal protection needed
3-5: Moderate – Hat and sunscreen
6-7: High – Seek shade and wear full protection
8+: Very High – Avoid exposure

What is Air Quality Index (AQI)?

0-50: Good
51-100: Moderate
101-150: Unhealthy for sensitive groups – wear mask
151+: Unhealthy – stay indoors
